it doesn’t have the sauce, the themes are half baked and sometimes contradictory, but the slice of life elements are always going to work for me and i liked some of the side characters. other side characters basically didn’t exist or weren’t worth including. was about 150 pages too long. unclear if the author does or does not hate fat people. some good quotes save this from an unfavorable review.
